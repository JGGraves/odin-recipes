Blueberry Simple Syrup

Description

Winter always has me missing the sunshine, so I opt to make lemonade to remind me of the hot summer afternoons. What's a good lemonade without a fresh fruit syrup to go with it? After making fresh lemonade for the millionth time, I knew I needed an extra punch to change it up. This blueberry simple syrup was just the right addition!

Estimated Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Blueberries (2 cups)
  • Sugar (1 cup)
  • Water (1 cup)
  • Lemon juice (1 teaspoon)

Steps

  1. In a medium pot, bring blueberries, water, sugar, and lemon juice to a light simmer
  2. While the mixture is warming up, mash blueberries with a wooden spoon or masher
  3. Let the mixture simmer for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, then remove from heat
  4. After the syrup has cooled to a handling temperature, pur it through a sieve into a sealable glass container
  5. Store this syrup in the fridge for up to two weeks
