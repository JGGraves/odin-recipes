Taco Soup

Description

With this dump and go recipe, winter meals are a breeze. In under an hour you too can have this hearty, warm, and flavorful meal!

Estimated Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1lb)
  • Taco seasoning
  • Miced garlic (1 tablespoon)
  • Onion
  • Diced tomatoes with green chiles (1 can)
  • Whole kernel corn (1 can)
  • Black beans (1 can)
  • Pinto beans (1 can)
  • Tomato sauce (1 can)
  • Beef broth (16 oz)
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, and cumin to taste
  • Shredded cheddar, sour cream, and advocado for finishing touch

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef on medum-high burner
  2. Drain excess grease from beef and cook down the diced onion for 5 minutes
  3. Once onions are translucent, add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Add the browned ground beef and stir in taco seasoning
  5. Once the meat and onion is coated, add in all canned ingredients and beef broth
  6. Allow the soup to simmer and reduce until the desired thickness, adding salt, pepper, paprika, and cumin to desired taste
  7. Serve with shredded cheddar, sour cream, or advocado on top
